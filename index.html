FAQ

Common questions

Is this actual legal advice?

No. ClearSign is a legal information tool, not a licensed law firm. We help you understand what you're signing and flag potential risks — but for complex situations or high-stakes contracts, we always recommend consulting a qualified lawyer.

Which provinces and states do you support?

All 10 Canadian provinces, 3 territories, and all 50 US states. We have specialized knowledge for Ontario, BC, Quebec, Alberta, and all major US jurisdictions. Just tell us which province/state governs your contract.

What types of contracts can you review?

Employment agreements, NDAs, freelance/consulting contracts, lease agreements, service agreements, vendor contracts, SaaS terms, partnership agreements, and more. We're adding new document types regularly.

Is my document data private?

Absolutely. Documents are encrypted in transit and at rest. We never train on your documents, and you can delete your data at any time. We're PIPEDA and SOC 2 compliant by launch.

How accurate is the analysis?

In our beta tests with practicing lawyers, ClearSign identified 94% of material risks in standard commercial contracts. We're continuously improving. Our model is validated by Canadian and US legal professionals.
